Pennsylvania High School Lacrosse - Spring-Ford overwhelms Pottsgrove

April 16, 2026: Pottsgrove, PA 19464

Fans at Thursday's league test between the Spring-Ford Rams (Royersford, PA) and the host Pottsgrove Falcons (Pottsgrove, PA), witnessed Spring-Ford's lacrosse squad top Pottsgrove by a count of 15-5.

The Rams (8-4) will now prepare for their test against Easton Area (Easton, PA). The Red Rovers enter the non-league test with a 14-0 record. In their last test, Easton Area thwarted Moravian Academy (Bethlehem, PA), 9-7, in a non-league test.

Next, the Falcons battle the Upper Merion Area Vikings (King of Prussia, PA) in a PAC-10 test, on Tuesday, April 21. Pottsgrove will attempt to move on its 1-5 season record.
